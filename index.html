How To Play

Game Rules

  1. Set up players (minimum 2)
  2. Generate a prompt and a word
  3. Players take turns completing the challenge
  4. The Game Master awards points for creative answers
  5. Play continues until you decide to end the game
  6. The player with the most points wins!

Example: If the prompt is "Come up with a fast food restaurant with this word in its name:" and the word is "DUCK", you might say "Duck Burger King" or "McDuck's".
